A disproportionate number of “Best Paper” and “Most Interesting Paper” selections use qualitative data. Yet, there is a confusing range qualitative methodologies and myths about what effective qualitative papers should look like. This PhD seminar is centered on the opportunities and the challenges of qualitative research. In this course, we will focus on four central qualitative methods used across the fields of strategy and organization theory – case study, ethnography, historical analysis, and grounded-theory building.
